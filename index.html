**************** Begin of Salesforce Case Transfer Format **************

1) Is this a 24x7 support request:

Yes or No - If yes provide a rationale, and any expectations
and/or special request of the L3 support team. The 24x7
requests are severity 1 tickets only and are intended for
high business impact and production down issues including
the full 24x7 availability of the customer as well.


NOTE: 24x7 designation aims to put higher weight on a request
for support, and spans the entire work force involved in
responding to it. It is not meant as a directive placed on
any one individual or team.

2) Statement of the problem:

Describe the problem as detailed as possible. Please also provide
business impact this issue is having on customer, especially for
severity 1 issues.

3) Is the problem Reproducible? yes, by customer

Yes or No - If yes define who can reproduce it (L2, L3,
customer only or a combination of that).

4) Reproduction steps:

Clearly describe the step by step instructions how to
reproduce the problem. Add links to available documentation
or information about the test machine where the reproduction
case is available and running.

5) Product(s) version (incl. Fixpack Level) / DBMS Product & Version:

Define the exact release levels of the BPM product and related
back-end systems involved (like the database, ECM system or others).
Add a link where to find the detailed version information.

6) L2 Engineer analysis:

Describe the analysis from L2 side and which exact assets (PI, TWX,
logs, traces, dumps and others) were used and what the findings are.

7) MustGather info - Logs and Traces:

Add links to all relevant assets on ECUREP (logs, traces and so on).
In case of a Blue Diamond ticket define clearly where the related
assets are stored on the Blue Diamond network shares.

8) Have you had a peer review by another L2?

Yes or No - If yes who was the reviewer, if no define why.
